How we choose our picks

We don’t lab‑test harnesses ourselves. Instead, we spend hundreds of hours gathering and cross‑referencing long‑term owner reviews from verified buyers, professional dog trainers, and independent gear reviewers. We pay particular attention to patterns: which models develop loose stitching after three months, which clips break under normal use, and which harnesses owners keep buying year after year. We also look at the fit dossier for each harness, which body shapes it suits, how easy the buckles are to operate one‑handed, and whether the manufacturer stands behind the product with a solid warranty. Our goal is to flag honest trade‑offs (like a great no‑pull harness that may not fit a barrel‑chested pit bull) so you know exactly what you’re getting before you click “buy.”
