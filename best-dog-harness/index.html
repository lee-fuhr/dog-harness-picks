Quick answer

The Ruffwear Front Range is the best dog harness for most owners, offering a great balance of comfort, security, and durability.

A good harness is one of the most important pieces of gear you’ll buy for your dog. It makes walks safer, more comfortable, and gives you better control, whether you’re training a puller or just strolling through the neighborhood. The right fit prevents chafing, keeps your dog from slipping out, and protects their neck in case of sudden lunges. Choosing the right harness comes down to your dog’s size, shape, and walking style. Look for adjustable straps around the neck and chest, consider front vs. back clip options, and pay close attention to padding and breathability. A harness that fits well and matches your daily routine will make walks more enjoyable for both of you.
